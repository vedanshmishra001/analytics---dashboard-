# analytics---dashboard-
This project presents an interactive E-Commerce Sales Dashboard built to analyze revenue, profitability, customer behavior, and regional performance across India. The objective is to extract actionable business insights from sales data and identify patterns in customer purchasing trends, product performance, and payment preferences. 
Key Performance Metrics
The overall business performance highlights:
Total Sales Amount: ₹438K
Total Profit: ₹37K
Total Quantity Sold: 5,615 units
Average Order Value (AOV): ₹121K
These metrics provide a high-level summary of revenue generation, operational efficiency, and profitability.

## Regional Performance Analysis
## Sales by State

Maharashtra leads with ₹102K in sales.

Followed by Madhya Pradesh (₹87K).

Uttar Pradesh (₹38K) and Delhi (₹23K) contribute comparatively lower revenue.

This indicates strong western and central region dominance, suggesting targeted expansion opportunities in underperforming states.

## Orders by City

The geographical map visualization highlights higher order density in metropolitan and tier-1 cities, indicating urban-driven sales concentration.



## Category & Sub-Category Insights
Quantity by Category

Clothing (63%) dominates total sales volume.

Electronics (21%)

Furniture (17%)

Clothing is the primary revenue driver in terms of volume.

##  Profit by Sub-Category

Printers (₹8.6K) generate the highest profit.

Followed by Bookcases (₹6.5K) and Sarees (₹4.1K).

Tables and Accessories contribute moderately.

This shows that while clothing drives volume, certain sub-categories generate higher profit margins.


## Business Insights & Recommendations

Expand marketing efforts in underperforming states.

Promote high-margin sub-categories like Printers and Bookcases.

Reduce dependency on COD to improve cash flow efficiency.

Analyze reasons behind mid-year losses and optimize cost structure.

Introduce loyalty programs targeting high-value customers.
